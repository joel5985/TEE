About The Band

India's
Loudest
Secret

Best Kept Secret is a prominent Indian high-energy dance-rock and pop band based in Bengaluru. With a touch of Bollywood, a dash of Hollywood, a hint of retro, and a shout of pop — every performance is a masterclass in pure, unbridled energy.

Featured on the cover of GQ Magazine and recipients of the WOW Award for Best Band 2025 and Wedding Sutra's Best Live Act 2025, they've shared the stage with Hoobastank, Indian Ocean, and Poets of the Fall, and toured across Asia and Europe.

"These boys are amazing. They will make it big."

— Raveena Tandon, Bollywood Actress
