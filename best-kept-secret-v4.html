About Us

INDIA'S
LOUDEST
SECRET

Best Kept Secret is a prominent Indian high-energy dance-rock and pop band based in Bengaluru. With a touch of Bollywood, a dash of Hollywood, a hint of retro, and a shout of pop — every show is a full-scale experience.

Featured on the cover of GQ Magazine, winners of the WOW Award for Best Band 2025 and Wedding Sutra Best Live Act 2025. They've toured Asia and Europe and shared the stage with Hoobastank, Indian Ocean, and Poets of the Fall.
